Open Sourcing Mental Illness is a speaking campaign by Ed Finkler to raise awareness and understanding of mental illness in the developer community. Many of Ed's appearances are sponsored by Engine Yard's Prompt campaign.

If you're interested in having Ed speak at your conference, event, or user group, just drop him a line.
