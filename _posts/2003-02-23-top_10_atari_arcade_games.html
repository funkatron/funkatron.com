The last remnants of Atari Games, the coin-op developer that spun of from the console-making atari in 1984 (I think), died with the closing of Midway Games West this month. This inspired Gamespy to do a roundup on what they consider the 10 best Atari games to ever grace arcades. Since I’m all about never getting over my past, I loved this article. You should too.
