A couple weeks ago I was looking for a basic RSS Viewer widget for Konfabulator. Strangely, I couldn’t find one in their gallery; it was all stuff designed to view one or a handful of feeds. So I wrote my own. It has problems with a couple feeds I’ve run across (most notably the one for phaedo.cx), but for the most part it seems to function as it should.
