RSS Viewer now supports multiple feeds, and is available for download. From the readme:

You can enter multiple urls in the “RSS Feed URL:” preference, separated by “|” (pipe) characters. For example:

http://slashdot.org/slashdot.rdf|http://www.hardocp.com/hardocp.rdf|http://www.funkatron.com/mt/index.rdf

Items from the feeds are displayed in the same window by their order in their respective feed: item 1 of feed 1 is shown first, then item 1 of feed 2, then item 2 of feed 1, etc.
